The Novel Planner

Theme · Premise · Character · Story

Start from:

Working top-down from theme → premise → character → story

1 Thematic Territory
2–3 topics your story lives inside. These are nouns, not arguments yet.
Mortality Identity Power Grief Love Belonging Redemption Freedom Betrayal Truth War Faith Ambition Memory Coming of Age Transformation
2 Thematic Claim
What does your story argue about those topics? One sentence. Should be arguable — someone could write a great story that disagrees with it.
3 Premise
The "what if" that generates your story. A situation that puts maximum pressure on your thematic claim.
4 Central Character
Who is the right person to test your premise? Define them from the inside out.
Wound
Fear
False Belief / Contradiction
Want vs. Need
5 World & Setting
Where and when. What does the physical world press against the character?
6 Tone & Voice
What register does this story live in?
7 Supporting Characters
Who tests, mirrors, or opposes the protagonist?
Story Structure
8 Beginning
Opening World
Inciting Incident
9 Middle
Rising Pressure
Midpoint Shift
Crisis / Dark Night
10 End
Climax
Resolution
Final Image
11 Open Questions & Gaps
What don't you know yet? What is the story resisting? What are you most afraid of in this material?
